ℹ️ About BoltPocket

BoltPocket is a self-hosted Bitcoin wallet with NFC card payments. Tap your card to pay at any merchant that accepts Lightning.

🔑 Your Card

Your NFC card works like a contactless debit card, but for Bitcoin. Tap it at any point-of-sale that supports bolt cards or LNURL-withdraw.

⚡ Lightning Address

Anyone can send you sats using your lightning address: {{ ln_address }}. It works with any Lightning wallet — just like an email address for money.

📱 How It Works

  • Receive — share your lightning address or show the QR code
  • Pay in shops — tap your NFC card at the terminal
  • Send — scan a QR or enter an address, confirm with card tap
  • Save — set up recurring payments to stack sats automatically

🔒 Security

Every payment from your wallet requires a physical tap of your NFC card. No card, no transaction. Your card's keys are encrypted and never stored in plaintext.
